I am a PhD Candidate at the [Information Learning and Physics Laboratory](https://www.epfl.ch/labs/idephics/) at [École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL)](https://www.epfl.ch/en/), where I work at the intersection of statistical physics and machine learning. My research focuses on developing theoretical frameworks to understand the behavior of machine learning models, particularly in the context of high-dimensional statistics. I am fortunate enough to be advised by [Prof. Florent Krzakala](https://www.epfl.ch/labs/idephics/).

Outside of my research, I spend most of my time climbing.
